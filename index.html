A $2.8 million cure

A biotech firm wins FDA approval for a one-time gene therapy that effectively cures a severe inherited disease affecting roughly 3,000 U.S. children. Clinical results are dramatic. The list price is $2.8 million per patient, paid once. Payers, employers, and state Medicaid programs must now decide whether — and how — to cover it.
